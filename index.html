Nine pillars of Digital India

Digital Locker System

Minimize the usage of physical documents and enable sharing of e-documents

MyGov.in

Includes “Discuss”, “Do” and “Disseminate” approach.

Swachh Bharat Mission(SBM) Mobile App

Used by people and Government organizations for achieving the goals of Swachh Bharat Mission.

eSign Framework

allow citizens to digitally sign a document online using Aadhaar authentication

Online Registration System(ORS)

This application provides important services such as online registration, payment of fees and appointment, online diagnostic reports, enquiring availability of blood online etc..

National Scholarships Portal

end to end scholarship process right from submission of student application, verification, sanction and disbursal to end beneficiary for all the scholarships provided by the Government of India.

Digitize India Platform(DIP)

for large scale digitization of records in the country that would facilitate efficient delivery of services to the citizens.

Bharat Net

high speed digital highway to connect all 2.5 lakh Gram Panchayats of country

Next Generation Network



manage all types of services like voice, data, multimedia/ video and other types of packet switched communication services
